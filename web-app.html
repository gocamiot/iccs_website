Meeting Expectations

FROM START TO ... FULFILLING AN ONGOING PROCESS THAT MEETS EVERY BUSINESS NEED NOW AND IN THE FUTURE

The Website and Development Team at ICCS gathers business knowledge from key players within your organisation in order to provide a practical internal matrix of criteria and quality standards. Stakeholder input is a critical element in our process and crucial to the success of your project.

❓ THE RIGHT APPROACH

With a focus on defining the purpose, goal, and target users your business website and/or app will attract, we set about gathering the requirements that help us create the technical backbone that supports all pathways, at the same time, enhances aesthetic appeal and speaks the language of your audience. We’ll provide advice and come up with suggestions for discussion, while listening to you.

❓ THE RIGHT DESIGN

Discovery and intensity define a phase where we start forming frameworks to bring your business site/application’s user interface to life. Adaptability, commitment and understanding sit at the heart of creative process. We’ll provide prototypes to help you visualise where we’re taking the project. We call this user testing to gather feedback and refine your ideas within the design.

❓ THE RIGHT TECH STACK AND TOOLKIT

We’re at a phase where both design and development teams begin merging creative assets with development specs. The development team has decided on the appropriate technology stack – by now, they’ll have started designing the site/application architecture, defining the data model and database schemas, and creating technical specifications and documentation to support their decisions against your requirements.

❓ THE RIGHT PROCESSES

Every project of this nature reaches a quiet phase where the real work begins and the baton gets handed to teams behind the scenes, notably our developers. The development work is broken down into manageable sprints or iterations, and tasks are assigned based on particular skills and expertise. Version control systems are used to manage code changes and file versions. The latter stage in this phase is focused on integration with existing systems and third party services, and conducting various types of testing to ensure functionality, performance, and security.

❓ THE RIGHT TIME TO PRODUCTION

Once all elements align and everyone’s satisfied, we set our sights on launching the final product. At it’s point, the site/application is deployed onto production environment and made available to end-users. This involves preparation and rollout tasks over and offsite areas, enabling deployment scripts, and planning the launch and follow-up schedules. You’ll be able to see your product, and provide us with feedback, along with requesting changes to be made.

❓ THE RIGHT SERVICE

Ongoing maintenance and continuous improvement involves monitoring performance and usage metrics, gathering data, enhancing product content, performing regular maintenance activities, and planning for future initiatives and enhancements. One of the cornerstones to the wellbeing of your project are service providers with a reputation for excellence and client retention.

Best Practices

The Future of Business Application Development for ICCS

As technology continues to evolve at a rapid pace, business application development must keep up with emerging trends and opportunities. We apply ourselves to keeping up with trends and best practices, which include:

RISE OF PROGRESSIVE WEB APPS (PWAS)

PWAs are web applications that provide a native app-like experience through features such as offline functionality, push notifications, and home screen installation. PWAs offer the benefits of both web and mobile apps, making them an attractive option for businesses looking to reach a wider audience.

USE OF Artificial Intelligence AND Machine learning

AI and ML are becoming increasingly integrated into business applications to enable intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and personalized user experiences. AI-powered chatbots, recommendation engines, and fraud detection systems are just a few examples of transformation.

EMERGENCE OF IMMERSIVE TECHNOLOGIES

Augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) are finding new applications in business contexts, from virtual product demonstrations to immersive training simulations. As these technologies become more accessible and affordable, we can expect to see more AR/VR enabled business apps in the future.

EMPHASIS ON ACCESSIBILITY AND INCLUSIVITY

With growing awareness of the importance of digital accessibility, business applications will need to be designed with inclusivity in mind. This means following accessibility guidelines, providing alternative input methods, and ensuring compatibility with assistive technologies.
